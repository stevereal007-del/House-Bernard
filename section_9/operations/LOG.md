## Log Format

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
OPERATION: [Codename]

THREAT ID: [S9-T-XXX]
THREAT LEVEL: [S1-S4]
ATTRIBUTION: [A1-A4]
AUTHORIZATION: [Director / Crown — date and method]
WEAPONS DEPLOYED: [S9-W-XXX, ...]
TARGET: [Description — no PII unless A1 confirmed]
OBJECTIVE: [What we intended]
OUTCOME: [What happened]
PROPORTIONALITY CHECK: [Was response proportional? Y/N + reasoning]
COLLATERAL: [Any unintended effects]
LESSONS: [What we learned]
STATUS: [ACTIVE / COMPLETED / ABORTED]
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
