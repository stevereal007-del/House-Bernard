## Retention Schedule

Per Charter Section V:

- Routine intelligence: purged after 90 days
- Significant intelligence: retained 1 year, annual review
- Critical intelligence: retained indefinitely, annual Crown sign-off

The Forgetting Law applies. Intelligence that is no longer
actionable is destroyed, not archived.
