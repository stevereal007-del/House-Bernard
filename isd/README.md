# Internal Security Directorate (ISD)

**Classification:** Operational details are CROWN EYES ONLY.
**Authority:** Internal Security & Intelligence Court Act

The ISD is House Bernard's counterintelligence organ.
Section 9 watches outward. The ISD watches inward.

See `INTERNAL_SECURITY_ACT.md` in the repository root for
the full legislative framework.

## Public Information

- The ISD investigates internal threats under Intelligence
  Court warrant authority
- Every investigation requires judicial authorization
- The Intelligence Court publishes annual statistical
  reports and redacted legal opinions
- Citizens may petition the Intelligence Court if they
  believe they are subject to unlawful investigation
