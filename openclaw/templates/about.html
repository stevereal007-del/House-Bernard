He applies the same discipline to a different kind of engineering: building algorithms that maintain coherence under the extreme pressure of context compression.

House Bernard is not a startup. There's no venture capital, no pitch deck, no growth hacking. It's one person's conviction that context rot — the fundamental problem that AI systems lose coherence over time — can be solved through adversarial testing, economic incentives, and the rule of law.

The name is the founder's family name. The House is the institution built to outlast any individual, including him.

The Research

Context rot

Every AI system operating over time faces the same constraint: context windows are finite. As memory accumulates, the system must compress — and compression destroys information. The question isn't whether information is lost. It's whether the right information is preserved.

Context rot is unintelligent compression. Critical state decays. Behavioral coherence degrades. The system forgets what matters and remembers what doesn't.

House Bernard attacks this by testing artifacts under extreme memory pressure. The Selection Furnace doesn't ask whether your code works — it asks whether it works after being compressed to 10% of its original state while processing adversarial inputs.

Survivors carry genetic material — algorithmic patterns for intelligent compression and self-auditing — that gets extracted and catalogued into a growing library available for any AI system that needs to maintain coherence over time.

The Agent

AchillesRun

House Bernard's sovereign agent. AchillesRun manages the Airlock, runs the Selection Furnace, maintains the Treasury, and coordinates research operations. It operates on OpenClaw as a peer node — other agents interact with it directly.

AchillesRun uses a Bicameral Mind architecture: local models handle routine operations while cloud-tier models validate critical decisions. This keeps costs low and latency manageable on modest hardware.

AchillesRun is not a chatbot. It's a load-bearing component of House Bernard's infrastructure, operating 24/7 under the same constitutional rules that govern every other participant.

Technical

Infrastructure

The entire operation runs on a single mini-PC. This is a design choice. Small infrastructure means small attack surface, low costs, and the discipline of building systems that work under constraint.

Compute
Beelink EQ13
Ubuntu 24.04. Dedicated hardware. The entire House runs here — agent, Furnace, Treasury, governance.
Security
Defense in depth
AST-based static analysis catches threats at parse time. Docker sandboxing with seccomp profiles. No network access for artifacts. Every submission is scanned before a single line executes.
Agent Runtime
OpenClaw + Ollama
Local models for routine operations. Cloud validation called sparingly. The Bicameral Mind keeps costs manageable while maintaining quality on critical decisions.
